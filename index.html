Who We Are!

The Vonavana Tours and Travel is a locally-owned travel company based in Zanzibar, passionate about sharing the island’s beauty, culture, and hidden gems. With deep roots in the community and expert knowledge of the region, we craft personalized experiences that go beyond the ordinary so you can discover Zanzibar like never before.

Our team is made up of passionate travel professionals and local guides who know every corner of the island—from the winding alleys of Stone Town to the untouched beauty of our remote beaches and spice farms. We specialize in crafting tailor-made tours that match your interests, whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, cultural exploration, or a bit of everything.

At Vonavana,

we believe that travel should be more than just sightseeing—it should be a connection. That’s why we focus on authentic, meaningful experiences that let you engage with the people, traditions, and landscapes that make Zanzibar truly unique.

Let Vonavana Tours and Travel be your trusted companion on your journey through Zanzibar. With our local knowledge, attention to detail, and personal touch, your dream trip is in the best hands.
